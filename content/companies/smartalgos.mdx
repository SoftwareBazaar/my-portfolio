# Mission

SmartAlgos exists to give independent traders and prop desks the same calibre of infrastructure as bulge-bracket counterparts. We orchestrate AI-powered research, execution-grade automation, and custody workflows under one roof.

## Products

- **SmartAlgos Trading Platform** — Institutional trading OS with HFT bots, EA marketplace, research studio, and multi-asset coverage.
- **SmartAlgos Desktop** — Electron companion delivering always-on market intelligence, notifications, and strategy controls.
- **Insight Lab** — Managed quant research service with analyst briefs, economic calendars, and data partnerships.

## My Role

I architect the platform, train machine learning models, ship production code, and interface with regulators, partners, and clients. I also lead documentation and investor relations collateral.

## Impact

- Onboarded cross-border clients in the US, Kenya, and the wider African market.
- Processed thousands of automated orders with audit-ready logging and multisig escrow.
- Published market intelligence that helps clients navigate volatility with confidence.
